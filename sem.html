About Us

Welcome to Delighted service , where we believe in the power of food to nourish, uplift, and unite communities. Founded in 2023, our mission is simple yet profound: To reduce food waste and fight hunger by connecting surplus food with those in need. At Delighted service, we understand that hunger knows no boundaries and affects individuals and families from all walks of life. That's why we've created a platform that bridges the gap between food donors and organizations serving those experiencing food insecurity. Our dedicated team works tirelessly to ensure that no edible food goes to waste. user-friendly platform, food donors can easily upload information about available donations, while partner organizations can browse listings and request pickups based on their specific needs. By facilitating this process, we're able to maximize the impact of every donation and make a meaningful difference in the lives of individuals and families facing hunger.Join us in our mission to create a world where no one goes to bed hungry.
